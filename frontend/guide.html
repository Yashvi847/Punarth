♻️ Punarth Waste Segregation Guide

🟢 Dry Waste

Plastic, paper, cardboard, glass

How to handle: Clean and send for recycling

🔵 Wet Waste

Food waste, vegetable peels, fruit waste

How to handle: Compost or dispose in wet waste bin

🔴 E-Waste

Batteries, mobile phones, wires, chargers

How to handle: Take to authorized e-waste center

♻️ Golden Rule

Always separate waste at source: Dry + Wet + E-waste
